Trekoholic is a passion driven effort backed by wanderers who prefer treading down off-beat terrain and conquering less chartered territories. Traveling is our passion and over the years we have conquered a number of road-trips and treks and amassed data based on our experience and not just from the internet.
 
Based on our experiences and the plethora of stories to tell this website has been envisioned as an effort towards bringing fellow travelers and trekkers from across the country to contribute their experiences and suggestions so that the data can be used as a guide for anyone who has been traveling or planning to set up an adventure.
 
To summarise the website is an effort to provide information on the following:
  1. Know-How's about trekking
  2. Recommended vendors for various logistical support
  3. Information about what to do and what to look for in a particular destinaion
  4. Share stories and any kind of information that would benefit the community
Sourabh Upadhyay is the co-mastermind behind trekoholic. He and his brother and co-mastermind Shwetank Upadhyay have chartered various roadtrips in their beloved "Wanderer" (a modified Royal Enfield Bullet). Sourabh has been an active roadtripper since 2007 and has embarked on various roadtrips from Leh-Ladakh to Tawang, Bhutan and so on and including treks like Kedarkanta, Stok-Kangri.
Shwetank Upadhyay is the other co-mastermind. He is an avid roadtripper and trekker who has conquered Leh-Ladakh, Tawang, Spiti, Stok-Kangri and Kedarkanta and many more. His idea of relaxing during weekend is to undertake a short journey of 300+km. Shwetank has been an active roadtripper since 2007 and has embarked on various destinations from Leh-Ladakh to Tawang, Bhutan and so on.
